Our Love Story

When they say that you're meant to cross paths with a certain person at a certain time because it's meant to be, that's exactly what happened to us. We are two souls working hard to leave our past behind, to move on, to start something new.

We never knew that one message would change our lives forever. Things weren't easy from the beginning; life tested our relationship early on. There were many times we wanted to give up on each other, but our love and commitment kept us together

In September 2020, we officially became a couple, and the rest is history. The road that led us here wasn't easy; it was bumpy, amd rough, but in the end, we still found each other. My love, You are my best friend, my comfort, my home. I'm blessed to spend a lifetime with you.

"Every long-lost dream led me to where you are. Others who broke my heart were like the northern star, pointing me on my way into your loving arms. This much I know is true: God blessed the broken road that led me straight to you."
